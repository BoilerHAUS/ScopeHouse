Project name:
ScopeHouse

Core idea:
ScopeHouse is a renovation operating system. It helps homeowners and renovation professionals plan, run, and track home renovation projects from first idea to final closeout.

The product must be good enough for a serious homeowner, while structured enough that a professional general contractor, renovation consultant, or design-build firm could also use it.

Primary product vision:
Create a software platform that brings renovation planning, scope control, budgeting, scheduling, change tracking, documentation, communication, and closeout into one system.

Primary product promise:
Plan the renovation.
Price the renovation.
Run the renovation.
Track the renovation.
Finish the renovation.

Strategic product position:
Do not treat this as generic construction software.
Do not treat this as an enterprise ERP.
Do not treat AI as the main product.

Treat this as a renovation planning and control platform with pro-grade structure and clean usability.
